Welcome to NeighborHub

where we aim to restore the voice of your community

through listening.

Who are we?

NeighborHub is a place where technology connects neighborhoods and communities through the unique power of listening. Used as a tool for conversation, our goal is to bring people together through the means of values, empathy, and empowerment.

What Are We Trying to Solve?

With the current political climate being very divisive, our team believes having conversations with other individuals in a non-partisan way reveals that we are more alike than we are different.

We hope that this tool is used to facilitate conversations about a brighter future.

Here at NeighborHub, we believe that

it’s time to give the people back their voices.
